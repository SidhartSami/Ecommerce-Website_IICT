About Ozone

Discover the story behind Pakistan's leading men's fashion brand, crafted for the bold and the stylish.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Ozone emerged from a shared vision between two friends, Ahmed and Siddharth, who met at FAST University. United by their passion for fashion and innovation, they set out to redefine men's style in Pakistan.

Ozone is more than a clothing brand—it's a lifestyle for the modern, spirited youth who embrace trends in fashion, music, and culture to express their individuality.

From sleek streetwear to sophisticated casuals, our curated collections blend comfort with cutting-edge design. With 10+ stores across 4 cities and a thriving online platform, Ozone delivers quality and style nationwide.

Our Mission & Goals

Innovate Style

Push the boundaries of men's fashion with bold designs and trend-setting collections that inspire confidence.

Sustainable Fashion

Commit to eco-friendly practices, using sustainable materials to create clothing that’s kind to the planet.

Empower Expression

Enable every individual to express their unique style through versatile, high-quality wardrobe essentials.
